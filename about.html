A few years back, following my high school graduation, I started off my post-secondary education in business. A few months in, I realized it was not my calling. I then made my way into the art field, where I felt passionate and fit right in.

I am a recent grad, for the second time, from Algonquin College’s Graphic Design program. My first diploma was in Photography. I’m an UI/UX enthusiast. Who says a complex and powerful tool needs to be just as challenging or difficult to learn and use — and that is where I can add value: to make things simple and user friendly. I also enjoy illustrating and exploring motion graphics… Oh and I have a slight obsession with minions.

So all in all I'm just an average person in search for a full-time challenge.

Have any other questions? Don't be shy, feel free to shoot me an e-mail at hello@shaayaanjameel.ca.
