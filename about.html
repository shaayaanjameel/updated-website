In 2010 I started the my postsecondary education at Carleton University. A few months into my program (Business), I realized it wasn't the right path for me. I completed my first year and decided I needed to move towards the art field.

June 2016

The time had finally come, the time to graduate. I was excited and extremely happy that I had made my way to where I felt I belong and was the perfect fit for me. As part of my program I was required to complete an internship, which I had secured at Arctic Empire. I learned a lot and got to work on multiple projects such web design, ui/ux and branding. It was a great experience and I am very grateful for the opportunity.
